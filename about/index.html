About Pawstool

Pawstool is being built as a matrix of real online tools, not thin SEO pages. The platform starts with a durable static foundation and grows through researched batches.

Our operating principles

  • Publish real tools only, never fake converters or placeholder pages.
  • Prefer local browser processing whenever possible.
  • Use unique metadata, canonical URLs, structured data, and useful internal links.
  • Keep the sitemap limited to pages that meet quality standards.
