FAQ

What is title capitalization?

Title capitalization is the practice of capitalizing important words in a headline or title.

How do I check title case?

Paste the title into the checker and review which words should be capitalized or left lowercase.

Which words should not be capitalized in a title?

Short articles, conjunctions, and prepositions are often lowercase unless they appear first or last.

Can I use this for SEO titles?

Yes. It is useful for cleaning page titles, blog titles, and SERP-style headlines.

Is title case different from sentence case?

Yes. Title case capitalizes major words, while sentence case usually capitalizes only the first word and proper nouns.
