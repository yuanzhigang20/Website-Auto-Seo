FAQ

What does a robots.txt tester check?

It helps review whether robots.txt rules may allow or block crawler access to important URLs and whether sitemap hints are present.

Can robots.txt remove pages from Google?

Robots.txt controls crawling, not guaranteed indexing removal. For removals, use noindex or Search Console removal tools where appropriate.

What is a dangerous robots.txt mistake?

A rule like Disallow: / for the wrong user-agent can block an entire site from crawling.

Is this private?

Yes. The pasted robots.txt text is processed in your browser.
