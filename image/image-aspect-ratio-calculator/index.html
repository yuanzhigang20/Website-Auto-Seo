FAQ

How do I calculate image aspect ratio?

Divide width and height by their greatest common divisor to simplify the image dimensions into a ratio.

What is the aspect ratio of a photo?

It is the proportional relationship between photo width and height, such as 1:1, 4:3, 3:2, or 16:9.

How do I simplify width and height into a ratio?

Enter the dimensions and reduce both numbers by the same factor until they form the smallest whole-number ratio.

What are common image aspect ratios?

Common ratios include 1:1 for square images, 4:3 for classic photos, 3:2 for cameras, 16:9 for video, and 9:16 for vertical posts.

Can I use this for social media images?

Yes. It helps compare your image dimensions with common social media and web layout ratios.
