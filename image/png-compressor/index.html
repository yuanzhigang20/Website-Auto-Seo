FAQ

Which quality should I choose?

Start around 70-80% for photos, then reduce quality or max width if you need a smaller file.

Why is PNG sometimes larger?

PNG is lossless and works best for graphics and transparency. For photos, JPG or WebP is usually smaller.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes. Modern mobile browsers support the local image processing used here, though very large images may take longer.
