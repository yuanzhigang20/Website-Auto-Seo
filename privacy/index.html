Privacy Policy

Pawstool is designed around browser-first processing. Future image, PDF, and text tools will clearly state whether files stay local in your browser or require server processing.

Our operating principles

  • Publish real tools only, never fake converters or placeholder pages.
  • Prefer local browser processing whenever possible.
  • Use unique metadata, canonical URLs, structured data, and useful internal links.
  • Keep the sitemap limited to pages that meet quality standards.

Browser-first processing

Many Pawstool tools are designed to run directly in the browser. That means pasted text, simple calculations, formatting operations, and many file checks can happen locally on your device. When a workflow needs different handling, the page should explain the behavior clearly.

Safe input habits

Do not paste passwords, private keys, access tokens, confidential customer records, or regulated personal data into any online tool unless you have reviewed the page and your own security requirements. For normal public or low-risk content, the tools are intended to make quick cleanup and preparation easier.
